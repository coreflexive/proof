The $\leq$ relation on the natural numbers is reflexive.

●	$\textbf{Reflexive} \; \N \; \leq$

≡		Definition of reflexive

​	$\forall(x \in \N : : x \leq x)$

≡		Establish $\forall$

​	●	$x \leq x$

​	✓	$x \in \N$

​	≡		Definition of $\leq$

​		$\exists(k \in \N :: x + k = x)$

​	⇐		$\exists$-Introduction

​		$(x + k = x)[k := 0]$

​	≡		Textual substitution

​		$x + 0 = x$

​	≡		Identity of +

​		$\text{true}$

​	$\square$

⋯	$\forall(x \in \N : : \text{true})$

≡		All true

​	$\text{true}$

$\square$
